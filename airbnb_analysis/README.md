\# Airbnb Market Analysis: Columbus vs New York



\## Author

Lorna Wodzak



\## Project Overview

This project analyzes Airbnb listing data to compare the Columbus, Ohio and New York City markets. The goal is to figure out what locations and traits make Airbnbs popular, including host behavior, holiday traveling, and the aesthetic appearance of the Airbnb.



\## Research Questions



1\. What areas of Columbus and New York City have the highest concentration of Airbnbs?

2\. What is the median number of years hosting Airbnbs for Superhosts in this area?

3\. Does the aesthetic appearance of an Airbnb affect availability?

4\. Does the aesthetic appearance of an Airbnb affect quality reported in reviews?

5\. How do holidays impact Airbnb bookings?
